

## Ingredients:
| Ingredients    | Amount |
| -------------- | ------ |
| Cognac | 1.5    |
| Orange liquer  | 1.0    |
| Lemon Juice     | .5       |

### Garnish
Lime Twist	
Sugar Rimmed Coupe
### Instructions:
Combine ingredients in shaker filled with ice.
Shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds
Double Strain into chilled coupe.
